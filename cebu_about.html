ABOUT US

We are not just your typical “gym”, My Body Fitness is a full service-result based gym and fitness center. We inspire people to commit themselves through health, fitness and hard work. We offer stronger fitness programs, specialized amenities, equipment and smaller group class exercises. We are not a place of intimidation our gym staff and fitness instructors are happy to serve you and inspire you for making the decision to change your life for the better!


We are open from 6 am to 12 midnight and for your security and safety purposes, we have 24 hours surveillance cameras in all areas of the gym. You can join online for your convenience or call us for inquiries. Whether you are new to working out or you are an experienced athlete, there is something to be earned here if you are willing to put in the work. Join us now!
